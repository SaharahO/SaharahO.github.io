Night-sky Bunny

This was my second art piece I ever made in digital art. I started it off on a sketch paper that I used during my time in my art studio and wanted to make it digital to add more color and story to it. The story behind this was a bunny that my older sister had its name was Bonnie and I made it about her bunny because it seemed as she described her always want to run around. This is based around a bunny being free within the night to be themselves and just run around as much as they want.

Blue Women

The idea of this painting came from a show that I watch. I made this because it expresses the unviversal emotion of sadness. I believe it's one of the main important emotions to feel and what truly makes someone human. It's also an emotion that I believe most people reject trying to feel however this emotion is unavoidable and truly allows people to stand out making them their own Diamond. (The women in the image is Blue Diamond from Steven Universe.)

Recreation of: Viva la Vida

I gain this idea of recreating Viva la Vida by looking through past artist works and what they have done. This one cuaght my eye the most because it was created by Frida Kahlo and this was considered her very last art piece. I wanted to capture her idea of the painting and what it meant to her. Which was her love for life even through her struggles and Viva La Vida means "Long Live Life." ~ Credit: Frida Kahlo
